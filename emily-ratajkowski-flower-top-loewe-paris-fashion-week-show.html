Emily Ratajkowski Is a Flower in Bloom at Loewe's Paris Fashion Show

September 11, 2024
blog

Emily Ratajkowski was the embodiment of spring when she attended Loewe's show in Paris.

Today, the model was among the stars who sat front row at the fall/winter 2023–24 show, and for the occasion, she dressed head to toe in the house's creations. Her top was a piece of art in itself—a giant faux anthurium flower in bright greens and hot pink, which was fastened to her body with sleek metal straps painted green. Ratajkowski wore the vibrant piece with low-rise, wide-leg black pants and beige sandals.

Jacopo Raule//Getty Images

She let the fashion speak for itself, wearing no jewelry and keeping her glam natural and unfussy. For makeup, she opted for bronzy shadow, pink blush, and a brownish nude lip; and for hair, the My Body author simply wore a messy topknot with blown-out strands framing her face.

Jacopo Raule//Getty Images

The look stemmed from Loewe's spring/summer 2023 collection, which centered on the ideas of the fake and the natural, and how the lines often seem blurred. The anthurium flower, per creative director Jonathan Anderson's show notes, was "a product of nature that looks like an object of design and [was] treated as such," therefore becoming one of his main focuses for the collection.

Ratajkowski praised the designer on her Instagram after the show and filled her Story with photos and clips of her in the striking floral look—including one in which she's seen holding hands with fellow front-row VIP Naomi Campbell.
