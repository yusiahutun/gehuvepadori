Country Song Power Rankings January 2023

February 20, 2024
blog

The No. 1 country song to begin 2023 proves that there is more than one way to "make it" in country music.

Taste of Country's monthly Top 40 list has undergone a makeover. Instead of just listing the songs in reverse order like we always have, we've pulled data to help you better follow your favorite songs. Check out the improvements below.

Bailey Zimmerman, Lainey Wilson, Jelly Roll and Morgan Wallen are among the artists with two songs in the Top 40. Kane Brown's latest single continues to creep toward No. 1, while Cole Swindell holds on to a Top 10 placement. Find your favorite song below and click any link to listen.

This list of Top 40 country songs of January 2023 is compiled from Billboard chart data, streaming data, sales figures, staff opinion and reader feedback.

Top 5 Country Songs of January 2023:

1. Zach Bryan, “Something in the Orange” — A surprise live album and an everyman's initiative will bring positive attention to country music's most enigmatic and hottest singer. — STREAMING LEADER!

2. Kane Brown with Katelyn Brown, “Thank God” — Kane Brown's duet with wife Katelyn Brown has zipped up to the airplay Top 10 to begin January 2023, matching its rankings on sales and streaming charts.

3. Morgan Wallen, “Thought You Should Know” — Morgan Wallen needed a change. This ballad finds him covering new ground with as much (or more) sincerity as his previous hits.

4. Hardy Feat. Lainey Wilson, “Wait in the Truck” — The edgy lyric of this sales leader isn't putting off streamers or radio programmers. It's a future No. 1 on this list. — SALES LEADER!

5. Lainey Wilson, “Heart Like a Truck” — This is arguably the better of two Lainey Wilson songs at country radio, even if sales and streaming numbers are just behind her duet with Hardy.
