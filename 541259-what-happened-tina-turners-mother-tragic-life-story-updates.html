Who was Tina Turner?

Tina was a renowned personality in the American music sector. She wore the hats of a singer, songwriter, actress, and author. She began her musical journey in the hands of Ike Turner before becoming a solo artist.

After a hiatus, she launched one of the greatest comebacks in history. She is one of the best-selling music artists, with over 100 million records worldwide. After years of sickness, Tina died on May 24, 2023, in Küsnacht, Zürich, Switzerland. Her body was cremated after a private funeral service.

Who was Tina Turner's mother?

Tina’s mother was Zelma Priscilla Bullock. She was born on November 18, 1918, in Brownsville, Tennessee, United States. Her parents were Joe Currie and Georgianna Flag.

The celebrity mother married Floyd Richard Bullock. The couple settled in Nutbush, Tennessee, where Zelma's husband worked overseeing sharecroppers. They were blessed with two kids, Alline Bullock and Anna Bullock. Zelma’s daughter Anna adopted the stage name Tina Turner.
