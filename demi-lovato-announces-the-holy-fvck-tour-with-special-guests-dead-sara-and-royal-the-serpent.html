Demi Lovato Announces the Holy Fvck Tour With Special Guests DEAD SARA and Royal &amp; the Serpent

March 03, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Grammy-nominated and polarizing singer/songwriter Demi Lovato announced Tuesday (June 7) their upcoming Holy Fvck fall tour, named after her forthcoming eighth studio album, which is set for release on August 19, 2022.

The 32-date Live Nation produced tour kicks off Tuesday, August 30th in São Paulo’s Espaço das Américas hitting San Francisco, Montreal, Nashville, and more before wrapping up at The Pavilion at Toyota Music Factory in Irving, TX on Sunday, November 6th. DEAD SARA will support the North American part of the tour with Royal & the Serpent on select dates.

“I’m so excited to get back on the road after four years without touring, and even longer since I’ve toured in South America,” said Demi Lovato. “We’re working so hard to deliver an incredible show for all my fans and I can’t wait to see them in person to celebrate this new music.”
