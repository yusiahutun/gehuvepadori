Turns out, the Playboy models were there to watch Anderson and Hefner get it on, but Pamela was having none of it. Elsewhere in the interview with Piers, the former Baywatch star claimed that being vegan is “better for your sex life”.

She also spoke to The Sun about her diet and revealed: “Being vegan is an aphrodisiac diet. It’s a win-win. Meat makes you impotent and unhealthy.” Anderson also gave some dating tips and admitted that relationships are more than the physical element.

Pamela thinks that people “need somebody to love both” as “the mind is the most erotic part of the body”. Anderson then said that if someone can “stimulate her mind,” then her “body follows”.

She then said: “I really believe in committed relationships and finding love. Everybody’s a rock star now, so I think the cool thing is to be married or in a committed relationship. It’s the nouveau sexy thing to do - to not follow the her in accumulating notches on your belt.”

The actress has been married five times - first to arguably her most famous ex, Tommy Lee. The pair were married from 1995 to 1998 and their infamous sex tape leak was the topic of the 2022 drama series Pam and Tommy.

After that, she married musician Kid Rock, then to Rick Salomon twice before she rekindled with her former flame Jon Peters before finally marrying her bodyguard Dan Hayhurst in 2020. They split in 2022.
