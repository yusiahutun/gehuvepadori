Meet All Kris Jenner's Grand-Children and Youngest Members of the Kardashian-Jenner Clan

July 16, 2024
blog

Since their first foray into the spotlights over eighteen years ago, Kris Jenner’s family has expanded so rapidly it has become too challenging to keep up with the Kardashians.

For years, fans watched reality star Kris Jenner navigate life as a mother of six, struggle through her divorce while managing her kids and grooming them into world-famous superstars and business moguls.

Since the world got a whiff of this one-of-a-kind supermom on their family reality show, “Keeping Up With The Kardashians,” a lot has changed within the family.

From their ever-increasing entrepreneurial ventures to the dramatic expansion of the clan, thanks to the youngest additions to the family since her kids embraced parenthood.

Notably, the star has a handful of grandchildren from her brood of six. In addition to being the ever-valiant momager of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, Kris Jenner has also proven herself as a firm mom and a doting grandmother who always makes Christmas more than just a feast of jingle bells for her little ones.

Keep reading to discover all you need to know about Kris Jenner’s numerous grandchildren.

Kourtney Kardashians’ Kids

Kourtney Kardashian is the oldest daughter of Kris Jenner and Rob Kardashian. Coincidentally, she was also the first of their brood of four to become a parent.

Now, the 42-year-old is a mother of three adorable kids, Mason, Penelope, and Reign, who she shares with her ex-partner Scott Disick.

1. Mason Dash Disick

Kourtney’s first child, Mason Dash Disick, was born on December 14, 2009. The moment the reality star discovered her pregnancy was captured on their family reality show, “Keeping Up With the Kardashians.”

Months later, fans also experienced the thrill of a lifetime when they witnessed the baby’s birth live on the reality show. His middle name, Dash, is a nod at the family patriarch’s nickname.

2. Penelope P. Scotland Disick

Three years after welcoming their first child, Scott and Kourtney gave birth to their second child and only daughter Penelope “P” Scottland Disick. 

Like her brother, Penelope’s birth was also filmed on the show. Years later, the memorable moment the mother of three pulled the beautiful baby girl out with her own hands remains etched in viewers’ hearts.

Penelope’s middle name is a nod at Kourtney’s Scottish ancestry while her nickname, P is a short form of her first name. The 8-year-old has been featured on her parent’s social media over the years and appears to already have a favorite cousin in North Kardashian-West.

3. Reign Aston Disick

Reign Aston Disick, Kourtney’s second son landed on the planet on December 14, 2014, the same day his brother clocked five.

The world got the first glimpse of the youngster at four months old. Since then, he has remained a constant fixture in the Kardashian family photos, videos, and Kris Jenner’s Christmas celebrations.

Kim Kardashian West’s Kids

The second of Kris Jenner’s kids, Kim Kardashian West is famous for her stint on the family show, as well as her multiple businesses and relationship with estranged husband Kanye West.

Notably, she shares four children, North, Saint, Chicago, and Psalm with the rap mogul.

4. North West

Kim Kardashian’s first child North West was born on June 15, 2013, and bears the nickname, Nori. The fashion mogul’s pregnancy attracted vast publicity at the time, as her divorce from ex, Kris Humphries was yet to be finalized.

Additionally, it was a particularly difficult pregnancy, with the 41-year-old suffering complications including preeclampsia. The 8-year-old first appeared on “KUWTK” when she was almost two.

She has since stunned the world with her fashionable appearances and poise, proving herself a mini fashionista and beauty icon.

5. Saint West

Born on December 5, 2015, Saint West is Kim’s second child and first son. The Skims CEO first disclosed the boy’s name on Instagram two days after his birth.

At two, he developed pneumonia and spent three nights at the hospital undergoing treatment. He has since recovered fully.

6. Chicago “Chi” West

On January 15, 2008, Kim Kardashian somehow curbed the stereotype surrounding fertility struggles when she welcomed her third child via surrogate.

The adorable girl, Chicago West, nicknamed Chi has stunned the world with her princess attires and was first seen during Kylie Jenner’s first pregnancy announcement.

7. Psalm West

Kim and Kanye became fourth-time parents when they welcomed Psalm West on May 9, 2019, via surrogate. Kourtney revealed the surrogate was in labor during her appearance on “Ellen,” on the same day.

Two days later, the mother of four confirmed Psalm West as the calmest and chilliest of all her babies, revealing his name on May 18th.

Khloe Kardashian’s Babies

Kris Jenner has one grandchild, True Thompson, from her third daughter, Khloe Kardashian. Khloe welcomed the youngster in 2018 with ex-boyfriend Tristan Thompson after struggling for years to get pregnant for her ex-husband Lamar Odom.

8. True Thompson

True Thompson came into her parent’s life on April 12, 2018, four months after Khloe and Tristan announced their pregnancy.

The new mom disclosed her daughter’s name two days after her birth, revealing the name was a nod to her dad’s middle name and grandfather’s first name.

Rob Kardashian’s Daughter

The less-talked-about brother of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, Rob Kardashian has also contributed to Kris Jenner’s brood of grandchildren. He shares a daughter with his ex-partner, Blac Chyna.

9. Dream Renee Kardashian

Rob Kardashian dated dancer Black Chyna for months in 2016 before they finally became engaged.

Barely one month after announcing their engagement, the twosome gave the world another happy news in May 2016 as pregnancy speculations flooded the media.

In September, they confirmed the news, announcing they were expecting their first child together. Two months later, their daughter Dream Renee Kardashian arrived. Her birthdate was recorded as November 10, 2016.

Kylie Jenner’s Kids

Kris Jenner’s youngest daughter and second child with Caitlyn Jenner has broken many barriers since her foray into fame as a child.

While her elder sister, Kendall Jenner is yet to become a mom, she didn’t mind skipping the ladder. In fact, at 24, she is a mother of one, and a soon-to-be mother of two thanks to her relationship with partner Travis Scott.

Stormi Webster

Rumors of Kylie Jenner’s pregnancy first surfaced in September 2017. However, the then-teenager remained mum about her pregnancy.

She remained under the radar for months, finally announcing the birth of her “healthy baby girl,” Stormi Webster in February 2018. She also released a video detailing her entire nine months of pregnancy.

An Instagram post announcing the name of her daughter as Stormi Webster shared on February 6 became the most-liked post on Instagram at the time.

Stormi has since remained in the spotlight, capturing hearts with her cheerful nature and infamous annual Stormi world birthday parties.

Kylie’s Second baby

In September 2021, months after Kylie’s infamous breakup with Travis Scott and rumored reconciliation, Kylie revealed she was expecting her second baby with Scott.

She made the revelation by sharing a video of the positive pregnancy test, and reactions of various members of the family on Instagram.

Although the baby’s due date, gender, and name are yet to be revealed, one thing is certain – the Kardashian-Jenner family is set to become even bigger.
