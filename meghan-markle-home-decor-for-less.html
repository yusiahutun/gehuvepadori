Meghan Markle's home is beautiful - as seen in the Harry & Meghan Netflix documentary

    Classic yet modern, with simple, clean lines and a minimalist neutral palette. We could be talking about Meghan Markle's wardrobe but it’s also an apt description of her and Prince Harry’s home decor.

    The royal couple has given us several peeks into their Montecito home, revealing more in their new bombshell Netflix documentary, Harry & Meghan, which premiered on Thursday.

    We may not know for sure that Meghan is the mind behind the decor, but the house definitely has many of the same tasteful touches that were seen inside her former laid-back but sophisticated Toronto home.

    And don’t think you need a royal budget to recreate the Duchess's homeware style - you can shop lookalike pieces for a lot less on Amazon. We’ve hunted for bargain homeware and furnishings that would look perfect inside the family’s beautiful Santa Barbara mansion and its gorgeous garden.

    Prince Harry and Meghan Markle in their new Netflix documentary

    The key to copying Meghan and Harry’s abode? Keeping things fresh, airy and natural with linen, wood and glass accents, clean stripes, stylish coffee table books and a sense of calm… along with a bit of bohemian spirit (think macrame and aura-soothing crystals).

    Meghan has previously given us peeks inside her home during her virtual engagements and meetings

    Of course, make sure to pay attention to ALL the senses with scented candles and diffusers. Meghan’s favourites are by Diptyque, particularly the Baies scent. And of course, monochrome palettes feature highly, from striped decorative cushions to wall art. The result? A home fit for a Duchess!
