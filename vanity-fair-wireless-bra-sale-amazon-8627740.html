A 62-Year-Old Shopper Found the Most Comfortable Bra Ever, and It's Just $21

October 04, 2024

If you ask me, the hardest part of getting dressed each day is not putting together matching tops and bottoms; rather, it’s finding the right undergarments to wear with your outfit. Bras, in particular, often pop out or show through, ruining the look and poking your skin along the way. That’s why I asked undergarment expert Caralyn Mirand Koch for advice a few months ago, and she recommended the now-$21 Vanity Fair wireless bra for comfortable everyday wear. 

According to Mirand Koch, the bra is a “great smoothing piece” in both the “front and back” thanks to its wide band, high sides, and flexible, four-way stretch material. It has a subtle V-neckline, adjustable straps, sewn-in cups, and hook- and- eye closures on the back. Choose from 28 colors and sizes S through 3XL, which corresponds to bra sizes 34B through 44DD, per the brand.   

Hundreds of Amazon shoppers are fans of the Vanity Fair bra, too, and many of those people left glowing reviews. One shopper who wears a size 44D confirmed the bra offers “great support without any underwires” and has a “smooth look under T-shirts.” Another reviewer who wears a size 36DD confirmed there’s no “no jiggling or spillage” with the bra, while a third person who wears a 42D said the “straps don't cut into [their] shoulders, and the cups somehow keep [their breasts] up where they're supposed to sit.” 

Shoppers of all ages and life stages swear by the wire-free bra. A 62-year-old said it’s “possibly the most comfortable bra ever” and “by far the most flattering under clothes,” and a 64-year-old said there’s “no sagging” and “no smashing [their] boobs together.” Plus, a postpartum reviewer said the bra is “extremely comfortable while still being supportive and giving a beautiful shape.”
