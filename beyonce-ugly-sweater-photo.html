Beyonce Just Rocked the Best Ugly Christmas Sweater of All Time!

June 26, 2024

Yes, even Beyonce can make an ugly sweater look fab!

The "Drunk In Love" singer got into the holiday spirit on Monday night when she stepped out on the streets of New York City rocking one crazy Christmas ensemble.

The 34-year-old, who was headed to an office party, donned a striped sweater dress and a loud cardigan featuring everything from bows to tinsel and ornaments. The outfit even had little red stockings hanging off the back and, to top the whole look off, she accessorized with a Santa Hat, tree sunglasses and a festive red lip.

We're used to seeing the pop diva looking picture perfect, but we're absolutely loving her over-the-top X-mas ensemble!
