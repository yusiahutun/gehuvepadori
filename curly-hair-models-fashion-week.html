DKNY Babes

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Models Imaan Hammam and Jasmine Sanders take their trademark curls to the streets after the DKNY show in New York City.

Tousled Curls

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Hairstylist James Pecis created these loose kinks at the Zero + Maria Cornejo show with a Marcel iron, texturizing paste, and a little twisting and teasing.

3

Model-Off-Duty

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Model Alanna Arrington and her natural curls leaving the Akris show in New York City.

4

Runway To Real Life

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Model Noemie Abigail's hair was left basically untouched for the J.W. Anderson show, where the vibe was meant to evoke a cool little rich girl. On the streets of London, big, bouncy curls look just as cool.

5

Cloudy Curls

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Most of the models at Marchesa walked the runway with their hair slicked-back and straightened. But the models with curly hair were given a different treatment: almost none. Instead, hairstylists fluffed up their spirals with a little bit of hairspray to evoke the feeling of a cloud.

6

New Wave

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

It was all about the curl backstage at the Topshop Unique show in London. If a model had them naturally: awesome. If not, they were given the '80s supermodel treatment, which involved a ton of mousse and a teeny-tiny curling iron.

7

Well Red

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Model Madison Stubbington leaving the Anna Sui show with her burgundy eyeshadow and soft curls still in tact. 

8

Full-On

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

The secret to styling texture this thick is all in the haircut. Layers and layers chopped throughout will evenly distribute all the weight—so your curls can just do their thing.

9

Urban Hippie

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

If you've got 'em ('em being curls and waves), flaunt 'em. That was the philosophy backstage at Baja East, where hairstylists just enhanced models' natural texture with a little bit of Redken Diamond Oil.

10

Street Style

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Partially brushed-out curls and a 1,000-watt smile on the streets of London.

11

All Natural

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

It's impossible not to look cool with makeup and hair this good (these models had just walked in the Tome spring 2017 show).

12

Short and Sweet

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Women with curly hair so often think they can't have short hair. May we present Exhibit A., model Ari Westphale?  Cropped, curly hair is possible, and it's amazing.

13

Fuzzy Updos

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Creating evening hair with curls is as easy as twisting and pinning it up on top of your head, as seen at the spring 2017 Reem Acra runway show. 

14

Fringe Benefits

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Let model Mica Arganaraz at the Tory Burch show be your inspiration to allow your bangs to air dry once in a while. Or, if you have this curl type and are considering bangs—this is the sign to do it.

15

Loose Spirals

LAUNCHMETRICS SPOTLIGHT

Take a cue from model Yasmin Wijnaldum at the Vera Wang show: spiral curls and an off-the-shoulder neckline are a winning combination.
