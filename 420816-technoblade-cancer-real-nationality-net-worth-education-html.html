Did Technoblade die?

The news of Technoblade's death was announced by his father who appeared in an uploaded video on Technoblade's YouTube channel titled so long nerds. Technoblade's cause of death was cancer.

Technoblade's dad read a note that Technoblade wrote just eight hours before he passed away. In the note, Technoblade said that the money he makes from selling stuff and his videos would go to the Sarcoma Foundation of America and to help pay for his siblings' college.

At the end of the video, Technoblade's mom left a message in writing. Technoblade had wanted to make and present the video himself, but he couldn't because he was too sick.

The video was also planned to include the song Exitlude by the Killers, but it didn't due to potential copyright concerns.

What type of cancer did Technoblade have?

Technoblade never specified his cancer type, but it is rumoured that he had died of metastatic sarcoma. In 2021, he was away for a while, and on August 27, he uploaded a video titled Where I've been. In that video, he revealed that he had been diagnosed with cancer earlier that month.
