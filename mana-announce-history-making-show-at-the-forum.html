Man Announces History Making Seventh Show at The LA Forum

May 08, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Due to overwhelming demand, Latin Rock superstars Maná have announced a historic seventh show at The Forum in Los Angeles, CA as a part of their highly anticipated “Rayando El Sol Tour.”

The 35-date trek is scheduled to kick off August 31st in Laredo, TX and make stops in Houston, San Diego, Chicago, Miami, Brooklyn and more, before wrapping December 7th with the newly added date at The Forum in LA.

A spokesperson for The Forum said: “Maná makes history today by becoming the first artist that will perform seven shows in one year at the ‘Fabulous’ Forum since our reopening in 2014. Maná also holds our record for most shows since the reopening at 11. The Forum is proud and honored to be the home to Maná’s historic run of shows.”

“Maná is a true touring powerhouse,” said Bob Roux, President of U.S. Concerts for Live Nation. “The fan demand for their tickets puts Maná in the rarified realm of the absolute biggest bands in the U.S. Everyone at Live Nation feels so proud and very privileged to have had the opportunity to play a small part in what they have accomplished.”

Having formed in Guadalajara, Mexico in 1986, the quartet of Fher Olvera, Alex González, Sergio Vallín and Juan Calleros have gone on to be global envoys for the Latin Rock genre. In the United States, Maná holds an impressive nine #1 albums and ten #1 singles – more than any other band in the history of Billboard’s “Hot Latin Songs” chart.

Tickets for the newly added seventh show at LA’s Forum go on sale to the general public beginning this Friday, August 16th at 10am local time at LiveNation.com.
