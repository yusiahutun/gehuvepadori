Early life

Nicholas Perry, alias Nikocado Avocado is a Ukrainian-born American with an East Slavic heritage. He was born on 19th May 1992(31 years as of 2023) in Kherson, Ukraine. He was adopted by an American couple when he was still young.

The YouTuber learned of his adoption when he was still a child and struggled to understand his biological parents' decision. As a result, Perry acted out and received mental health problems as a child.

He has two siblings, a younger brother and a sister, with whom he was raised in Pennsylvania, USA. He has yet to reveal the identity of his biological parents, siblings, or adoptive parents.

Nicholas attended his elementary and high school in his hometown in Pennsylvania. After graduation, he joined The Pennsylvania Governor's School for the Arts in 2008 on a full scholarship and obtained a certificate of excellence.

He attended Chamber Music in Grantham from 2009 to 2011. In 2010, Perry attended The Catholic University of America, where in 2012, he earned a music degree in violin performance.

Career

After completing his college studies, Perry started his music career by playing the violin as a freelancer and home depot worker between 2011 and 2012. He then moved to New York City in 2013 to pursue the dream of playing in a Broadway orchestra.

The personality found it challenging to make a living as a musician surrounded by other talented ones in the city. In early 2014, Nicholas Perry abandoned his music career, transitioning to online content creation. The star created his first main YouTube channel on 27th May 2014.

He started content creation as a full-time youtube vlogger, where he concentrated on creating a vegan food-related lifestyle. He posted his first vegan mukbang video in 2016 when he was 150 pounds. Nikocado did this for a while until he was diagnosed with vitamin B12 deficiency, rotting tooth, and hypoglycemia leading him to quit veganism.
