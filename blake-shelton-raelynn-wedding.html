Blake Shelton Canceled Show So He Could Attend RaeLynn's Wedding

April 26, 2024
blog

Talk about the ultimate wedding present!

When RaeLynn was planning her wedding in 2016 to now-husband Joshua Davis, she wasn't sure if her former The Voice coach and mentor Blake Shelton would be able to make it. But he managed to pull through on her big day — and it was a surprise!

"I didn't think Blake was going to get to go, but Blake cancelled his show in St. Louis, Missouri to come to my wedding. And the sweetest part is he didn't tell me," RaeLynn tells Taste of Country Nights' Sam Alex. "I found it out later and they were like, 'He canceled his show to go your wedding,' and I was like, 'He better, I'll kick his butt,'" she jokes.

RaeLynn and Other Country Ladies Are Killing It!

RaeLynn and Davis wed surrounded by 300 family and friends, including Shelton and his girlfriend Gwen Stefani at their ceremony two years ago, and while there were other artist friends who were unable to make it due to prior engagements, RaeLynn knows everything worked out exactly as it was supposed to.

"It's so hard because there's so many of us artists that travel all the time, but the people that are meant to be there are meant to be there. That's what I always say in those situations," she explains. "It's hard to plan around everybody's schedules, especially when everybody's an artist, but the people that are meant to be there are going to be there."

RaeLynn's latest single "Queens Don't" is currently climbing the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.
