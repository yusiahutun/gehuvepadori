Married At First Sight stars Jules Robinson and Cameron Merchant are expecting their second child.

Jules, 43, made the announcement in an interview with this week's Stellar Magazine, saying she and Cameron, 40, couldn't be happier after several years of trying to get pregnant. 

'We've been trying since our son Ollie was one and he is now three-and-a-half. We were going into 2024 thinking, "If it doesn't happen, we will look at IVF",' she explained.

'But I never lost faith that my body could do this again.'
