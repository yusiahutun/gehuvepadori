Peta Murgatroyd Shares Photo Of Maksim Chmerkovskiy Rockin Around The Christmas Tree Naked Se

June 22, 2024

If Peta Murgatroyd‘s Christmas wish was for beau Maksim Chmerkovskiy to appear nude next to the tree, then Santa came early this year! The Dancing With The Stars professional shared the brilliant photo of her dancer husband decorating for the holidays while dressed in his birthday suit. How cheeky — literally!

In the photo shared to Instagram, Maks can be seen standing behind the beautifully decorated Christmas tree. “I wouldn’t normally share a picture of such content,” Peta wrote, “but I walked out of the kitchen and saw this #NotMadAtIt #TopOfTheMorningToYa #YoureWelcome.” See the hysterical photo below!

Apparently, Maks had something to say about this. “What?!” the 38-year-old Ukraine native replied. “I can’t put on finishing touches on Christmas tree ornaments at 6:30 in the morning naked in my own damn house?!?” However, Maks wasn’t the only one with a snarky reply.

DWTS Champion and NFL player Rashad Jennings asked, “You putting the pickle on top of the tree this year?” While TV star Carson Kressley wrote, “Your package arrived early!” Tons of other friends and fans joked and laughed at the epic photo, but we mean, how could you not?

This snapshot is all laughs and giggles, but for Maks, Christmas is extremely sentimental. Especially since the couple welcomed their first child, Shai Aleksander, in Jan. 2017. On Dec. 2, Maks shared an adorable photo of the almost two-year-old standing in front of the unadorned tree.

“I remember standing just like this. Not quite all the way back to when I was 23 months, but as long as I could remember I absolutely loved this time of year AND OUR TREE,” Maks captioned the post. “There’s so much I feel and want to say but I think everyone who’s a new parent knows exactly where I’m coming from.”

Peta and Maks welcomed their little bundle of joy on Jan. 4, 2017, and then tied the knot just a few months later in July. With the holiday season in full swing and romance in the air, we wonder if the couple is devising up a plan to have baby No 2!
