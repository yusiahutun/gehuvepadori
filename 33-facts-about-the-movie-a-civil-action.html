A: Yes, A Civil Action is based on a true story. It is inspired by the book of the same name written by Jonathan Harr, which tells the real-life legal battle between W.R. Grace and Beatrice Foods against the residents of Woburn, Massachusetts.

Q: Who stars in A Civil Action?

A: A Civil Action boasts an impressive cast. The movie features John Travolta as Jan Schlichtmann, the lawyer representing the families affected by water contamination, and Robert Duvall as Jerome Facher, the opposing counsel.
