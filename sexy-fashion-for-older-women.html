Cher

With a career spanning over six decades, Cher became a style icon with her barrier-breaking looks over the years. Now at 71 years old, Cher's not giving up her sexy on-stage looks anytime soon. On her 2017 tour and during a performance at the Billboard Music Awards in May, the singer hit the stage wearing the same skin-baring costumes she rocked in the '70s, '80s, and '90s—and proved she's still got it.

Jane Fonda

Just months away from her 80th birthday, the legendary Oscar-winning actress takes fashion risks on the red carpet now more than ever. With cut-out dresses, off-the-shoulder styles, and form-fitting bodysuits, Fonda proves women can have fun with fashion at every age.

Iman

When it comes to sexy style, a body-hugging dress isn't the only way to go. Supermodel Iman shows just how sexy pants can be with a sparkling version made for standing out.

Madonna

Getty Images

Two years shy of turning 60, the Queen of Pop isn't backing down from the boundary-pushing fashion she's rocked throughout her career. At the 2016 Met Gala, she made waves after showing up in a sheer dress that showed off her thong bodysuit. The singer defended her outfit choice on Instagram, writing: "The fact that people actually believe a woman is not allowed to express her sexuality and be adventurous past a certain age is proof that we still live in an age-ist and sexist society. I have never thought in a limited way and I'm not going to start...If you have a problem with the way I dress it is simply a reflection of your prejudice."
