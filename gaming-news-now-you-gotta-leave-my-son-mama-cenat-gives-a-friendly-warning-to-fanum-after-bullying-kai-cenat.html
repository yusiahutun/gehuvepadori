The latest stream from Kai Cenat was a rollercoaster. He played Fortnite, showcased his gaming skills, and had a heart-to-heart talk with YourRAGE. However, the highlight of his stream surely has to be Mama Cenat’s warning to Fanum. This warning from Kai Cenat’s mom has left many fans amused and looking for more.

It is not the first time Kai Cenat and Fanum have taken opposite ends. Both members of AMP House are known for their banter and clashes. But the blazes of their recent friendly beef have reached Kai Cenat’s mom, and she had some remarks for Fanum.

Kai Cenat and Fanum clash live on the stream

Before knowing what Mama Cenat said, readers would like to know what happened between the two. On November 26, Kai Cenat was reacting to videos along with his viewers. But Fanum interrupted this session with a call, which led to a friendly banter between the two AMP House members. Both threw verbal jabs at each other, with both of them not backing down.

During the banter, Fanum said, “Why are you telling people I don’t go to the gym no more?” to which Kai Cenat responded, “Bro you don’t go to the gym no more.” This led to both of them clashing again, with Fanum making fun of Cenat’s height and Cenat criticizing Fanum’s weight.

This growing tension led Kai Cenat to seek comfort with his mom. After talking with Fanum, Cenat immediately called his mom to complain about Fanum’s bullying.

Mama Cenat shares her remarks about the clash

As soon as Kai Cenat’s mom picked up the call, Cenat complained to her about Fanum’s bullying. After listening, she said, “Fanum, let me talk to him.” Kai Cenat then turned on her call for his viewers to listen to, hoping the message would reach Fanum.

Mama Cenat said, “Fanum, if you can hear me right this is Mama Cenat. Now you gotta leave Kai alone alright or else you gotta deal with me. And I know you cannot deal with me!” However, later she also advised Kai to use some of his secret taekwondo training against Fanum.

In the meantime, fans are amused by Mama Cenat calling out Fanum. They are hoping to see a reaction from him, hoping he will also seek aid from his parents.
