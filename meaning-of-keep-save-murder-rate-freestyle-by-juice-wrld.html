Meaning of Keep Save (Murder Rate + Freestyle) by Juice WRLD

The Juice WRLD song titled "Keep Save (Murder Rate + Freestyle)" is a powerful and intense track that explores themes of violence, introspection, and personal growth. The lyrics delve into the artist's struggles with mental health, the impact of fame, and his reflections on the world around him. Through a combination of hard-hitting verses and catchy melodies, Juice WRLD creates a thought-provoking piece that resonates with listeners.One standout lyric in the song is, "I get higher than the murder rate, Shut the fuck up, I can't concentrate." This line introduces the theme of escapism and self-medication. Juice WRLD uses drug use as a way to cope with the weight of his emotions and the chaos of the world. The contrast between getting high and being unable to concentrate highlights the internal struggles he faces as his mind battles with his reality. This theme is further developed through references to addiction throughout the song.Another important lyric in the track is, "Your bitch swallow my dick, said she proud of me, No one knew me, now bitches surrounding me." This line speaks to the artist's newfound fame and the changes that come with it. It explores the theme of validation and the desire for acceptance. Juice WRLD is reflecting on his journey from obscurity to success and how it has impacted his relationships. This lyric delves into the theme of therapy through art. Juice WRLD acknowledges that his music is an avenue to channel his emotions and confront his struggles. It serves as a cathartic release, allowing him to heal and find solace in the creative process.A particularly imaginative theme that emerges from the song is the idea of personal transformation and growth. The repeated references to shooting and violence can be seen as metaphors for the artist overcoming his inner demons. The imagery of shooting like Stockton and pulling off in a high-speed Range Rover symbolizes Juice WRLD's journey to rise above his circumstances and achieve success despite the obstacles he faces. This theme of transformation encourages listeners to reflect on their own potential for growth and the ability to overcome hardships.In conclusion, "Keep Save (Murder Rate + Freestyle)" by Juice WRLD tackles complex themes through its lyrics. The song touches on the struggles of mental health, the impact of fame, and the power of artistic expression. Through clever and thought-provoking lyrics, Juice WRLD invites listeners to reflect on their own experiences and challenges. Ultimately, the song encourages personal growth, resilience, and the pursuit of inner peace in the face of adversity.
