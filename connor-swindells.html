Swindells started his career as a television actor. At first, he acted in an episode of the television series called Harlots in 2017. Further, he portrayed the role of Mostyn in the series. Similarly, he also acted in the British drama television series, Jamestown in 2017. 

The 23-year-old actor also tried his luck in the film industry. In 2018, he debuted in films with the film named VS., where he portrayed the role of Adam. Furthermore, his film, Emma, received a good response from fans and ended up earning $25.2 million against the budget of $22 million.

Connor made his career breakthrough after he starred in the British comedy-drama web television series, Sex Education. Well, the actor portrayed the role of Adam Groff in the series, which was first aired on Netflix in 2019.
