Burger King UK has launched a new-and-improved King Box - including the popular Double Melts Burger, fries and a drink with a new bonus addition of the highly coveted Doritos Chilli Heatwave Chicken Fries.

This almost-too-good-to-be-true new combo offers mega value, as customers can get their hands on the new Doritos King Box for just £5.99 in restaurants nationwide.

And - with a portion of the Doritos Chilli Heatwave Chicken Fries usually priced at £4.89 alone - this means burger lovers can upgrade to an entire meal for a great price.

The Doritos King Box also includes crispy golden fries and a refreshing drink - marking the most ambitious upgrade of the year.

There are also plenty more tasty discounts to tuck into on the Burger King® app, meaning it’s easier than ever to enjoy the best of Burger King for less.
