Nicki Nicole is an Argentine-born rapper and singer. At just 22, she achieved a lot with her name and fame in the industry. She has released a few self-made singles called “Wapo Traketero”, “Colocao”, “Mamichula”, and “Mala Vida”. Apart from her songs, she recently gained fame for her acting in Netflix’s La Firma.   

Early Life

Nicki Nicole was born on 25th August 2000, making her only 23 years old as of 2023. She was born in a middle-class family in Rosario. Not all celebrities are born with high-class mode or richness by birth. Some, like Nicki, would have to work hard since childhood to maintain their career in singing, where they had a keen interest. She went to Colegio Comunidad Educativa La Paz and received her graduate degree.

Nicki Nicole Career

Nicki Nicole began her career with certain small roles. Initially, she became part of the duo Jovani & Nicki Nicole and their music album in 2018. The duo is popular for releasing the songs like “Ahora Quiere Volver” and “No Puedo Seguir.” After making her strength in singing and developing her skills with the increasing demand of the industry or after working on a few short projects, Nicki Nicole released “Wapo Traketero” in April 2019.

In August (of the same year), she collaborated with the producer Bizarrap for her thirteenth release of “Music Sessions.” the song was very much liked by the audiences and peaked at number 3 on the Billboard Argentina Hot 100, making both artists reach the heights of popularity. Later that month, the Argentine artist released her second album, “Años Luz”. On 8th November 2019, Nicki Nicole had her debut with her album release, which was one of the productions of Bizarrap, and collaborated with Cazzu and Duki. the album was called RecuerdosII followed by the release of her other single, Diva.

Rise to Fame

On 28th October 2021, she presented another album, “Parte de Mí”, which made the path for her career, and she started working on other new songs. This song was the most delighted and praised because many artists featured in the album, including Delaossa, Dread, Rauw Alejandro, Mar-I, and Trueno. On 12th November 2021, Nicki Nicole joined Los Ángeles Azules and collaborated to release the latest cumbia hit, “Otra Noche.” Then on 3rd December 2021, she collaborated with Aitana and had the release her latest song, Formentera. Next, on 15th December 2022, she joined with other artists like Cris MJ, Standly, and Duki to release another song, Marisola Remix.
