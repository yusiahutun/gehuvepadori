Forget wearing your heart on your sleeve - Taylor Swift is wearing it on her wrist

September 09, 2024
blog

The internet’s favorite couple, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, are flaunting their love again after the singer was spotted wearing jewelry honoring his football number.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are the It couple of the moment, uniting sports and pop fans into one loved-up community. The duo made their relationship public after Taylor attended a Kansas City Chiefs game with her beau’s mom, Donna, in September. It’s pretty clear that Swift is head over heels for the NFL athlete as she was recently seen sporting a bracelet in his honor.

Taylor Swift wears her heart on her sleeve with Travis Kelce bracelet

Taylor is making clear to Travis that he Belongs With her by wearing a gold beaded bracelet with the number 87, Kelce’s football jersey number.

The Love Story singer attended the Chiefs game on Sunday (October 22) as the team played against the Los Angeles Chargers. Swift is clearly still in her Red era as she wore the team’s red merch jumper and a black cheerleader-inspired miniskirt – because she’s Travis’ biggest hype woman, of course. She paired the look with her signature rouge lipstick.

Here’s a closer look at the bracelet; the two middle white beads read “87”, while on either side are small hearts.

Sunday’s game was the fourth time Taylor attended one of Kelce’s games since making her debut on September 24, when they sparked romance rumors by leaving together.

She flew to New Jersey in early October with friends Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds, and Hugh Jackman for the tight end’s game against the New York Jets.

Travis Kelce’s father approves of ‘charming’ Taylor

The Cruel Summer singer, 33, has the Kelce parents’ seal of approval as they have both hung out with Taylor at football games. Swift was publically seen with Travis’ father, Ed Kelce, for the first time on October 12 as they chatted away mid-game.

Telling People about his first impression of Taylor, Ed called her a “charming, down-to-earth, and very smart woman”. He knew the Grammy-winning singer didn’t have a diva attitude after he saw her picking up litter in the stadium suite.
