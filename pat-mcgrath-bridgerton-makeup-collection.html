Bridgerton fans, you're going to love the Pat McGrath collection inspired by the hit Netflix show… 

    Move over Simon Basset and his spoon licking, for season two we're swooning over something a little different Bridgerton-related. 

    Legendary makeup artist Pat McGrath has teamed up once again with Shondaland and Netflix to create another gorgeous collaboration for the hit series Bridgerton. 

    In celebration of series two, and the mutual admiration between Pat McGrath and Shonda Rhimes, Pat created a modern, royal cosmetics collection that flatters every skin tone.

    While there is plenty in the collection, the Limited Edition Eyeshadow Palette has our heart.

    Pat McGrath said: “Like so many people, I fell in love at first swoon with Bridgerton. I created a Limited Edition Cosmetic Collection inspired by the romance and searing passion that we saw in Season one.
