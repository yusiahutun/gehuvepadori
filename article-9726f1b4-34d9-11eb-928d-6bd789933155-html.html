Danny Carol Newsome | Obituaries

April 02, 2024

Danny Carol Newsome, 65, of Laurel, Mississippi, passed away Tuesday, December 1, 2020 at Merit Health (Wesley) in Hattiesburg, Mississippi. He was born Friday, November 11, 1955 in Laurel, Mississippi. Graveside Services will be held Saturday, December 5, 2020 at 2:00 PM at Lawn Haven Cemetery and burial will follow in Lawn Haven Cemetery. Rev. Danny Stringer will officiate. Colonial Chapel Funeral Home of Laurel is in charge of arrangements. (601) 649-3342 Danny was a member of Pineview Baptist Church and a faithful coffee drinker at David’s grocery. He was a retired timber buyer for Leaf River. He was preceded in death by his parents, John H. and Lillian Newsome, brothers, Jeff Newsome, Johnny Ray Newsome, step-sister, Teresa Stockman. Survivors include his wife of 44 years, Susan Newsome, sisters, Linda Morrison, Amie Newsome Oliver (Chris), Delores Newsome Butler (Jerry), Brother in law, Brian Matthews, Sister in law, Sybil Atwood (Bo), 3 step-brothers, George, Anthony and Scott Martinez, 3 step-sisters, Cindy Rayner, Pam McPhail, Belinda Dearmon, 5 nephews, Robert Jordan, Scott Morrison, John Morrison, Dwayne Newsome, Matthew Newsome, 7 nieces, Kelly Barnett, Stephanie Jordan, Kim Lee,Trinity Lee, Kayla Jordan, Virginia Jordan and a host of great-nieces, great-nephews and friends. Pallbearers will be Scott Morrison, John Morrison, Matt Barnett, Jackie Lewis, Cricket Taylor and Ted Newsome. Honorary pallbearer will be Kirk Walters. Online guestbook may be signed at www.colonialchapellaurel.com
