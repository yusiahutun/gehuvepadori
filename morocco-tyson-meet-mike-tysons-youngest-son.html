If you love boxing, it’s impossible not to admire the legendary Mike Tyson or his kid, Morocco Tyson, who has won his father’s fans’ hearts.

The birth of Morocco Elijah Tyson on January 25, 2011, in a health facility in Henderson, Nevada, brought immense joy to Mike Tyson and his wife. Morocco Weighed 8 lbs., 13 oz., and measured 19 inches long at birth. Reports say he looked so angelic that he was described as a sweet and tranquil baby.

Mike Tyson’s representative told PEOPLE magazine, “Mike and Kiki are overjoyed to welcome their precious son into the family. Morocco’s parents weren’t the only ones thrilled at his birth. The entire Tyson fan club took a liking to the young lad and has watched him grow.

We take a look into the life of this 12-year-old.

Morocco Tyson Kids Choice Award

While most celebrity kids shy away from the limelight, Morocco seems not to mind, and neither do his parents, to an extent. In 2018, Morocco appeared on the Kids Choice Award orange carpet alongside his parents, biological sister Milian, and half-brother Miguel. The family had fun taking pictures while their father doted on his kids.

Morocco Tyson’s Half-Siblings 

Morocco is the youngest in the Tyson family, making him the seventh child with six older siblings. Of all his father’s children, Milian is the only child who comes from Morocco’s mother.  

Morocco’s eldest half-sibling Mikey was born in 1990. She is a product of Mike Tyson’s first marriage to Mikey Lorna Tyson. Rayna, also known as Ramsay Tyson, born on February 14, 1996, and her brother Amir born in August 1997, are Morocco’s second and third older siblings from his father’s second marriage to Monica Turner. 

Miguel and Exodus came from Mike Tyson’s relationship with his ex-girlfriend, Sol Xochitl. Sadly, Exodus, Morocco’s second older sister, is late. She died at 4 in 2009, long before Morocco was born and his father even met his mother. Exodus was caught in a treadmill cord in her Phoenix home and died after she was rushed to a local hospital. 

According to reports, Miguel, Tyson’s first son and Exodus elder brother, found her tangled up in the treadmill cord. According to ABC News, while playing, Exodus accidentally got entangled in the treadmill cord, which police described as acting like a noose.

Despite immediate efforts by her mother to rescue her and perform CPR, Exodus was unresponsive. Mike Tyson was informed of the situation and rushed to the hospital in Phoenix, where Exodus was on life support. Despite the medical team’s efforts, Exodus passed away at St. Joseph’s Hospital and Medical Centre.

Was Morocco Tyson’s Sister’s Death an Accident?

Following a thorough investigation by the Phoenix Police, it was determined that Exodus’ death was a tragic accident. According to Sgt. Andy Hill, she was playing on a treadmill when her head became entangled in a cord loop, rendering her unable to free herself.

The mother immediately called 911, describing her baby being choked. Mike Tyson expressed gratitude for the prayers and support he received at the time.

The incident prompted Consumer Reports to emphasize the importance of treadmill safety, describing the death as preventable. Mike Tyson referred to Exodus as his “Angel” and revealed to Oprah Winfrey that he chose not to know the details of her passing.

He went on to describe that his first thought after he received the news was that it was a tragedy. According to him, he was grateful he found a way to mourn his daughter without sinking back into his demons.

Morocco Tyson and Mike Tyson’s Relationship

Mike Tyson loves his children. However, the former boxer seems fond of his last two kids, Morocco. We have seen the renowned boxer pose in several pictures with Morocco, and the father-son bond is powerful. Based on Mike Tyson’s Instagram handle last year, they are golf buddies. 

Morocco doesn’t just share this beautiful bond with his father; he also shares a beautiful relationship with his mother. The beautiful pictures of Morocco and his mother online go a long way to prove this.

Who Is Morocco Tyson’s Mother?

Morocco’s mother is Lakiha Spicer Tyson. She is a fashion entrepreneur who married Morocco’s father in 2009. Lakiha was a Muslim but followed the Christian faith following her marriage to Mike Tyson.
