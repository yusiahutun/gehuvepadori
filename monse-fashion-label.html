Everything you need to know about the fashion label

October 01, 2024
blog

Courtesy of Monse

From Sarah Jessica Parker's Met Gala ensemble to Amal Clooney's recent outing, Monse is fast becoming a celebrity favourite – and it's not hard to see why. Here we divulge everything you need to know about the brand.

Who:

Laura Kim and Fernando Garcia, previously of Oscar de la Renta (with 18 years experience between them), started the label a year ago with an aim to create "modern and feminine" pieces. As well as Monse, both also work as full-time consultants for Carolina Herrera and were nominated for the 2016 CFDA emerging designer award.

What:

The brand's signature styles – which are all about deconstructing shirts – have been a hit with Sienna Miller and Amal Clooney. Meanwhile, Brie Larson wore a one-shouldered velvet gown to the Vanity Fair Oscar party this year (after picking up her Best Actress award), proving the label is winning over even the glossiest of red carpets.

Brie Larson and Sarah Jessica Parker on the red carpet

Why:

Structured, striking and remarkably fresh, the brand is sure to go from strength to strength in the next year. Whether you are picking up office wear or something to make a statement in after dark, we suggest this is a label to invest in.
