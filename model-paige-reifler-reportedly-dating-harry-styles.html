Model Paige Reifler admits dating Harry Styles

March 27, 2024
blog

Looks like Harry Styles is off the market. After weeks of speculation, model Paige Reifler has admitted that she is dating the One Direction band member. The New York model addressed the rumours that she was dating the floppy-haired heartthrob at the French Connection #NeverMissATrick bash.

Paige Reifler has admitted to dating Harry Styles

"I went to the Warner party at Shoreditch House with a friend and saw him there," she told a fellow partygoer. "The next morning I woke up and I was in all of the newspapers and magazines. I wasn't expecting that, it's not like that in New York." Asked directly whether she was in a relationship with Harry, she said, "Yes, I am seeing him."

But Harry isn't the only famous face to have been attracted to the pretty blonde – she revealed that Orlando Bloom has also showed an interest in her. "I went to see him in Romeo and Juliet and sat front row," she said. "His manager came and got me after the show and asked if I wanted to go backstage. I couldn’t believe it. We chatted and he said I looked as though I didn't enjoy it, but I really did!" The relationship didn't go much further than that, however. "We texted for a bit, it was after he'd split from Miranda Kerr. I didn't hear anything else from him. But yeah, I wish."
