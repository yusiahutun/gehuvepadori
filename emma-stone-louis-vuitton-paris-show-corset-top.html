Emma Stone Wears Corset and Mini Jacket to Louis Vuitton's Paris Show

June 13, 2024
blog

It’s nearly impossible not to gawk at Emma Stone’s outfits in her latest film, Poor Things, which were fabulously designed by Holly Waddington. From the leg-of-mutton sleeves to the ruffle blouses, Stone’s wardrobe as Bella Baxter is quite enviable—so it was a joy to see the actor embody her character Tuesday, on the last day of Paris Fashion Week.

Stone arrived at the Louis Vuitton show in a look from the host brand that included a cream corset top layered under a mini quilted puff-sleeve jacket (a layer Bella would certainly choose for the sleeves alone).

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

She paired these matching pieces with bright white high-waisted slacks that featured crisp creases at the top and at the bottom, where they engulfed Stone’s sandal shoes. To round out the look, the Oscar-winning star accessorized with golden rings and a boxy white clutch, which she held by its golden chain handle.

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

It was no surprise to see Stone at the show, given that she is an ambassador to the French fashion house and has been wearing the brand for years. Just a couple of weeks back, the Easy A star wore Louis Vuitton while attending the 77th BAFTAs, where she pulled out another dramatic sleeve. This creamsicle-colored gown featured an asymmetrical lacy top, which left one of Stone’s arms bare, while the other arm featured an oversize leg-of-mutton design. The sheer skirt was finished with a thick hemline that featured rows of puffy fabric.

Mike Marsland//Getty Images

So far this season, Stone has also worn Louis Vuitton to the Golden Globes and the Critics Choice Awards, and if we had to place our bets, we’d guess that she’ll wear the label to the Oscars this weekend, too.
