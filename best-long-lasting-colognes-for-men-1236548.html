    How to Choose the Best Long-lasting Cologne?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider while purchasing a top-rated cologne for men - 

    Notes: Always pay heed to the top, middle/heart, and base notes of the best cologne that you are planning to buy. This will help you pick the most ideal one as per your sensory preference. For a long or hectic day, you may choose a woody cologne that comes with notes of vanilla, musk, sandalwood, patchouli, and ylang-ylang. You can also choose to invest in notes that belong to a fruity or flowery family for your daily routine. 

    Sillage: Sillage is basically the trail created by the cologne that wakes you up as soon as you enter a room. If a passerby is immediately hit by the wall of fragrance, then that’s not a good sign. You need to invest in a good cologne for men that possess a very low or medium sillage. For those looking for a good long-lasting scent for office use, invest in the one that features a more subtle sillage. 

    Longevity: The durability or longevity of the cologne is largely associated with the concentration of essential oils. A long-lasting men's cologne usually has a 2 to 5% concentration that will still keep you fresh and fragrant throughout the day. Please avoid spraying way too much cologne to make it last as it will kill the originality of top notes and make you over-fragranced right at first impression. 

    Family of Fragrances: According to your sensory preferences and taste, you may splurge on top colognes for men only after analyzing their family of fragrances. There are woody, fruity, floral, citrusy, aquatic, and oriental families. Out of these, woodsy scents are highly-versatile, stronger, and spicier, making them ideal for day as well as nighttime use. For morning meet-ups, you can choose any good men's cologne with fruity, citrus, or floral notes. 

    After taking note of the above-mentioned aspects, get to know how to apply the best cologne for men to make it linger for a prolonged period of time.

    How Can I Make a Fragrance Last Longer?

    Below are the steps to follow to make a fragrance last longer on your skin - 

    Step 1: Take a shower. Cleanse your skin thoroughly with a shower gel or soap.

    Step 2: Moisturize your skin, especially your pulse points where you wish to apply your cologne. 

    Step 3: Apply the moisturizer onto your wrist, neckline, knees, and folds of your arms and legs. 

    Step 4: Spritz your cologne on the moisturized areas, collar bones, and the rest of the pulse points. 

    Step 5:  Do not rub it vigorously, let it stick to your skin naturally. 

    Tada, you are done!

    FAQ's

    What kind of cologne lasts the longest?

    Cologne with heavy and rich base notes lasts the longest.

    What notes last the longest?

    Base notes like the notes of sandalwood, cedar, musk, vanilla, and patchouli last the longest.

    What are top, heart, and base notes?

    Top notes are the notes that you sniff immediately, middle or heart notes are those which you smell after the top notes have evaporated, and lastly, the base notes are the notes that linger on your skin for a prolonged period of time.

    Which cologne is the strongest?

    Happy by Clinique Cologne is the strongest.

    Does expensive cologne last longer?

    Yes, expensive cologne lasts longer as they are crafted from high-grade ingredients and exotic notes.

    Where do men apply cologne?

    Men apply cologne on their necks, wrist, and the crease of their arms.
