What is the Shatter Me series about?

Shatter Me series plot is about Juliette Ferrars, a 17-year-old girl with a lethal touch that kills. Juliette is placed in an asylum when she was 14, before the start of the series, after accidentally killing a small boy with her touch.

While in prison, Juliette gets a cellmate, Adam Kent, seemingly immune to her power. At first, Juliette and Adam are distant, but after some weeks, they get closer to each other, and Juliette shows him around the asylum. Interestingly, Adam knew Juliette before the asylum and was in love with her.

Warner makes Juliette an offer to free her, but in return, she must torture any Reestablishment prisoners with her touch. It's revealed that Adam worked under Warner, the chief commander of sector 45 of the Reestablishment.

Later, Adam and Juliette escape from asylum and Warner – marking the beginning of a story that blends action, romance, and the fight for freedom all centred around Juliette.

How many books are in the Shatter Me series?

There are 11 books in the Shatter Me series. Five are novellas, and six are novels. There are also two additional books, but these are collections of existing novellas. They are titled Find Me (Shadow Me and Reveal Me) and Unite Me (Destroy Me and Fracture Me).

Is the Shatter Me series complete?

Yes, in April 2017, Tahereh Mafi announced that she would release three more books in the Shatter Me series.

The first one was Restore Me, published on March 6, 2018. The novella Shadow Me, narrated from Kenji's perspective, was released on March 5, 2019. Defy Me, the series' fifth book, was released a month later. The sixth and final book of the Shatter Me series Imagine Me was released on March 31, 2020.

Is the Shatter Me series the same story?

No, the Shatter Me series isn't the same story repeated across six books. It's a continuous narrative that unfolds throughout the series.
