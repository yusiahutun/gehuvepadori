Celebrity birthdays 13 May: Stevie Wonder and Robert Pattinson

March 29, 2024
blog

We are wishing a very happy birthday to everyone who is celebrating their day of birth today – 13 May. At HELLO!, we are taking a look at some of the famous faces who will be enjoying their birthdays with their loved ones - and plenty of cake and champagne hopefully! From a Hollywood heart-throb actor to a legendary singer, take a look to see who's celebrating today…

Happy Birthday Stevie!

Stevie Wonder turns 68

An enormous happy birthday to one of the most famous singers in the entire world – Stevie Wonder. Stevie will be 68 today and no doubt be spoilt rotten by his wife and his huge brood of children – five sons and two daughters. That's a lot of presents! The singer shows no sign of slowing down and his last performance to date was at the Equal Justice's Initiative's concert for Peace and Justice in Alabama. The event was live-streamed on Tidal and also featured glittering performances by Usher and Dave Matthews. The legendary singer told the audience during the performance: "I come to you with a deep pain in my heart and a great love in my soul. My hope is that we will come together as a united people, before it's too late."

Enjoy your day Robert! 

Robert Pattinson turns 32

Many happy returns to Robert! The actor, producer and model who is best known for his role of Edward Cullen in the Twilight film series will be celebrating his 32nd birthday today. He also played the role of Cedric Diggory in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire and starred in Water for Elephants and Maps to the Stars. The heart-throb is currently single after splitting up with long-term girlfriend FKA twigs in October 2017. The couple were engaged after Robert proposed in 2015. In February, Robert was seen spending time with former girlfriend Kristen Stewart in LA – causing Twilight fans to speculate that a reunion could be on the cards. Watch this space…
