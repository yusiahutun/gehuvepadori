Running Community Mourns Loss of Legendary Coach Mike Fanelli

The running world lost an iconic figure and coach with the recent death of Mike Fanelli at age 70 after a battle with brain cancer. Over 50 years, Fanelli motivated countless athletes to chase excellence while spearheading innovation as a running executive with Reebok and visionary magazine publisher.

Beyond his profound professional legacy modernizing the sport and business of running, Fanelli will be remembered by loved ones as a nurturing soul who found purpose in helping others achieve their highest potential.

Early Running Passion

Like so many lifelong runners, Mike Fanelli discovered the magic of testing physical and mental limits on area tracks and trails during his 1960’s Philadelphia youth. He quickly realized athletics provided a platform for growth when pushed furthest outside comfort zones toward fulfillment.

After showing promise locally, Fanelli earned an athletic scholarship to San Francisco State University - expanding competitive horizons and opportunities while laying the foundation for iconic career firsts still impacting the sport decades later.

Visionary Running Industry Leader

Fanelli’s breakthrough arrived in 1978 when he pioneered athlete sponsorship through Blue Ribbon Sports, planting early seeds for Nike. But his greatest lasting impact unfolded during a trailblazing tenure leading global marketing and promotions at Reebok throughout the company’s meteoric 1980’s rise to a top athletic brand.

Colleagues credit Fanelli’s innovations popularizing road racing, running apps and women’s performance footwear with propelling the activity from niche to international mainstream obsession and prosperity. His creative mark remains indelible.

Inspiring Coach and Mentor

Despite game-changing professional feats, Mike Fanelli counted his most fulfilling lifelong achievements as those spent personally uplifting runners of all abilities to reach untapped potential.

Through his Reebok Boston Track Club coaching and prolific RunnersWorld writing, Fanelli built a global platform for democratizing excellence advice. He cherished this access to change lives by sharing the keys for unlocking greatness he knew existed within every aspiring athlete if given an empowering spark.

Courage Facing Brain Cancer

When receiving a grim glioblastoma diagnosis prematurely ending his running journey, Mike Fanelli summoned the same resilient mindset adopted from years motivating others to beat daunting odds. He courageously continued pursuing ambitious mileage goals while clinging to possibility against all hope.

In the process, he personified the defiant human capacity to choose optimism on fate’s terms when confronted by limits beyond control. This grace facing mortality left one final template to inspire people in life’s depths.

Legacy Still In Motion

Those closest to Mike Fanelli will undoubtedly mourn losing a soulmate and nurturing force behind boundless personal fulfillment. But his legacy ensures the running community forever moves forward guided by the elevating ideals he embedded to advance lives well beyond his own.

The identity of modern running reflects Fanelli’s open, empowering vision crafted over years gradually reshaping perspectives of what inner potential means when awakened and shared. Through his voice, Fanelli still brings self-belief to every finish line crossed.
