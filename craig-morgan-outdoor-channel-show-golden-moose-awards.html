Craig Morgans Outdoor Channel Show Nominated for Eight Golden Moose Awards

April 07, 2024
blog

'Craig Morgan All Access Outdoors' has been nominated for eight Golden Moose Awards, the Outdoor Channel's awards celebrating creativity and hard work amongst its programs. Morgan's show -- which airs every Saturday morning at 11AM ET -- picked up three of those nominations in fan-voted categories.

In addition to Best Overall Series, Fan Favorite Hunting Series, and Fan Favorite Host, Morgan will be vying in the Best in Big Game, Deer, Blooper/Outtake, Turkey and Graphics categories. 'Craig Morgan All Access Outdoors' is the Outdoor Channel's top rated Saturday morning hunting show.

The 2011 season was Morgan's second season with the series. In addition to allowing access to his life in the woods, he also shares parts of his personal life, including his family and motocross exploits.

Fans can vote on Golden Moose Award categories until Dec. 26. According to his official website, Morgan will be taking the rest of the year off before returning to the road on Jan. 14 in Denver, Colo.
