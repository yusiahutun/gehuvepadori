11 Best New Fall 2021 Scents

blog

1

Fenty Beauty Fenty Eau de Parfum

1

Fenty Beauty Fenty Eau de Parfum

Credit: Courtesy

The long-awaited Fenty Eau de Parfum is finally here—although the first drop already sold out. If you can manage to get a hold of this magnolia-forward fragrance, it’s worth celebrating just like the megastar herself (that is, eating caviar for breakfast in bed).

2

Maison Margiela 'REPLICA' Autumn Vibes

2

Maison Margiela 'REPLICA' Autumn Vibes

Credit: Courtesy

The name says it all: With notes of red berries, cardamom, cedarwood, and moss, it smells like a walk through a (fancy) park on a fall day.

Gucci Flora Gorgeous Gardenia Eau de Parfum

4

Marc Jacobs Fragrances Perfect Intense Eau de Parfum

4

Marc Jacobs Fragrances Perfect Intense Eau de Parfum

Credit: Courtesy

If you love the original Perfect fragrance that came out last year, wait until you smell this sophisticated new take. It maintains the daffodil and almond notes, but incorporates night-blooming jasmine and sandalwood for a touch more warmth.

5

Coach Coach Dreams Sunset Eau de Parfum

5

Coach Coach Dreams Sunset Eau de Parfum

Coach’s new take on their Dreams fragrance is inspired by a road trip—a really, really nice-smelling road trip. With top notes of bergamot and pear, a heart of jasmine, and base featuring vanilla and tonka bean, it's as gorgeously layered as a, well, sunset.

6

Boy Smells Suede Pony

6

Boy Smells Suede Pony

Now 29% Off

The cult candle brand now has an equally obsession-worthy fragrance line. Intended to be worn and loved by all genders it has notes of suede (of course), along with hazelnut and cardamom, but the coconut water and pineapple keep it light.

7

Armani Beauty Terra di Gioia Eau de Parfum

7

Armani Beauty Terra di Gioia Eau de Parfum

Credit: Courtesy

The latest from Giorgio Armani has a little bit of everything. Grounded in Indian jasmine, it’s rounded out with sweet almond and fruity notes of bergamot, citrus, and pear.

Valentino Voce Viva Intensa Eau de Parfum

8

Valentino Voce Viva Intensa Eau de Parfum

A more intense, winter-appropriate version of last year's warm floral perfume, beloved by Lady Gaga.

Ralph Lauren Ralph's Club

Ralph Lauren Ralph's Club

This Earthy, woody scent is for all genders. It's the perfect scent for your next big night out.

Armani Beauty My Way Eau de Parfum Intense

10

Armani Beauty My Way Eau de Parfum Intense

With orange blossom, tuberose, and vanilla—this bright fragrance is a modern twist on classic and beloved notes.

HERMÈS Twilly Eau Ginger Eau De Parfum

HERMÈS Twilly Eau Ginger Eau De Parfum
