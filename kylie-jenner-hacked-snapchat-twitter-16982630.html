The internet nerd managed to get into the 19-year-old's Snapchat account where they threatened to release nude images of the Life of Kylie star.

The social media superstar employed the same blasé attitude when she was hacked only last year.

She said: "So my Twitter was hacked and I don't really care, I'm just letting him have fun."
