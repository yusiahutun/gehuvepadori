North West and Kim Kardashian Had The Most Adorable Mother-Daughter Photoshoot

August 02, 2024
blog

Kim Kardashian just shared a slideshow of photos featuring her budding fashion icon daughter, 5-year-old North West. The reality star said the photoshoot was both requested and creative-directed by North, which seems appropriate given who her famous parents are.

"Me & my bff! North came to visit me on set & said momma can we do a photo shoot together just me & you!" Kim captioned the series of images. "I followed her poses & direction so here it is! I always dreamed of having a baby girl & she’s perfect!"

The photos show the famous mother-daughter duo working it for the camera against a black backdrop, holding up peace signs, putting up signs of the horns, and sharing an adorable embrace.

Kim is sporting a white silk mini dress, long wavy hair, and bare feet in the pictures. Meanwhile, North looks equally stylish in a beige tulle dress, gold sandals, a little jewel necklace, and straightened hair.

North is clearly a Kim's mini-me. The KKW Beauty mogul recently appeared in a video with YouTube beauty guru James Charles, and discussed how much her daughter loves makeup. "She loves to have friends over and do makeup on dolls, on her...makeovers is her thing," Kim says. "She just likes to play makeover!"

Kim also shared that North loves wearing bright lips for special occasions. "She'll wear a lipstick color out and then she'll ask me 10 times 'Mom, is my lipstick still there?'" she explained.

We need a North West beauty tutorial immediately.
