B. SCOTT has seen their star soar and has become an inspiration to millions of followers.

But many fans have wondered how they started out and how they became famous in the first place.

Who is B. Scott?

Born in Virginia on April 21, 1981 40-year-old B. Scott is an American television personality, radio show host and internet celebrity.

They are best known for their YouTube videoblogs and their website, LoveBScott.com.

B. Scott is also a contributing editor to The Glam Network, and an Ebony Magazine advice columnist.

In March 2021, they told their followers: "I am a trans non-binary person and I use they/them pronouns.

"My gender identity, who I am on the inside, doesn't fit the binary labels of 'man' or 'woman'.

B. Scott is known for their celebrity interviews which includes Mariah Carey, Ne-Yo, Chaka Khan, Aubrey O'Day and Ashanti.

They have also appeared on shows like The Tyra Banks Show, Oxygen and BET.

In 2015, they launched The B. Scott Show podcast as a successor to their YouTube series of the same name.

According to the show's description: "B. Scott, the ‘Queen of Tea,' serves up the hottest tea in pop culture with lovebscott.com's editor Denver Sean."

When did they start LoveBScott website?

On January 1, 2007, B. Scott launched their LoveBScott.com which pop culture: celebrity news, fashion, music, nightlife, and entertainment.

The name of the website was selected in an effort to give it a readily-identifiable personality.

In May 2007, B. Scott started incorporating YouTube videos into their website to personally connect with readers.

They won the Best Video Blog by The Black Weblog Awards in 2007 and 2008.

Is B. Scott married?

Not much is known about B. Scott's dating history.

It is not believed they are currently married, or have ever been wed.

B. Scott is believed to be currently single.
