PMD Beautys Clean Acne Tool Is the Best New Way to Fight Breakouts

If starting a new-and-improved skincare routine is somewhere on your list of resolutions for 2024, it’s time to invest in a few great products. And, while our list of favorite lotions and under-eye serums is long, as far as tools go, we’re big fans of one in particular: PMD Beauty’s Clean Acne device, which is FDA-cleared and key to healing annoying breakouts.

We’ve loved this brand for a while —and are especially into all of its microderm tools — but PMD’s newest addition is a real game-changer. Clean Acne is deep cleansing, using SonicGlow™ Technology, AKA vibrations, to break down all the dirt and oil that’s sitting within your pores. Flip it over to the other side and you’ll find it also emits a medical grade, LED blue light, which is used to treat mild to moderate acne. Finally, this tool is made from silicone, which is not only means it’s hypoallergenic and waterproof, but also that there’s no need replace the brush a few weeks down the road. It’s easy to clean and able to be reused, rather than being disposible.

The best part is, this treatment isn’t at all time consuming. Once you apply your cleanser, you’ll move the vibrating Clean Acne device in a circular motion around your face before rinsing, patting dry, and using the blue light therapy side on dry, clean skin. The whole process takes about three minutes, and once when you’re done, you just plug it back into the charger until it’s time to use it the next day.

According to PMD Beauty’s site, the results have been nothing short of impressive. 91% of people who have used the tool said blackheads have diminished, while 69% said their breakouts decreased. Many said this tool also helped make their skin look brighter and reduced the appearance of pores.

PMD’s Clean Acne retails for $229, but if you tend to wake up with mild to moderate breakouts, that’s a small price to pay, especially once you tally up how much you’ll be saving on spot treatments and other acne creams that rarely work. Plus, you won’t have to stress and frantically try to cover up your pimples with makeup. For us, shopping this tool is a no-brainer.
