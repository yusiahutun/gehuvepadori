Top Twitch streamer Kaitlyn "Amouranth" Siragusa was stunned when she received a gift with £57,000 cash and a private bodyguard service.

The 29-year-old, who has more than 10,000 paid subscribers, opened the mysterious package on her livestream earlier this week.

In the clip, she sat on the floor while taking a look at the content inside the box.
