What do the lines on your face say about YOU?

May 22, 2024
blog

Face reading astrologer Dadhichi Toth has offered his guide to help you discover what every facial feature says about you.

The Australian expert believes the different lines on your face can reveal basic personality traits and determine your overall approach to life.

With more than 30 years experience in face reading and astrology, Dadhichi teamed up with The Body Shop to celebrate the launch of their revamped Drops of Youth Serum - and explain how our unique features are linked to various aspects of our life.

'You face reveals valuable information about your character and destiny, while the lines of your face, just like palmistry, can tell a story about your life,' he explained.

'Equally, divide the height of the forehead between the hairline and the eyebrows by an imaginary line. Should a forehead line appear in this very spot - call it solar - this is the starting point.'

So can your face lines tell your fortune or bring you good luck?

SOLAR LINE

Personality, energy and longevity

The solar line can be found in the centre of your forehead. 

If your solar line is visible and prominent, Dadhichi said this means your vitality is strong - but if it's not too overpowering or unbroken, you're destined for success.

'The line being visible and strong means your vitality and your ambition are notable,' Dadhichi told Daily Mail Australia.

MARS LINE

Energy and power 

The Mars line can be found immediately above the solar line.

If present, Dadhichi said this line drives great energy, courage and ambition. 

As a result, you may be prone to having fiery relationships, particularly if the line is broken.

JUPITER LINE

Wisdom, luck and wealth 

The Jupiter line sits higher above your forehead - just above Mars.

Dadhichi said the line represents a high level of integrity, wisdom and spirituality. If particularly prominent, you may acquire a large amount of money in your life.

'Having this line is fortunate,' he said.

VENUS LINE

Sociability, love and romance

The Venus line sits below the central solar line of your forehead.

'Venus represents beauty and love. It's extremely fortunate to possess this line. It brings great fortune into your life,' Dadhichi said. 

If other lines cross Venus, he explained it may result in 'riches and possibly fame'.

MERCURY LINE

Communication, intelligence and imagination 

Sitting directly below the Venus line, the Mercury line indicates great intelligence, communication and social skills if well formed.

'Mercury is the planet of communication and ideas so in turn your communication and social skills are noteworthy,' Dadhichi said.

An upward curve of this line is a 'marking of luck in your life'. But if the line is broken into three parts, this could mean you have not only eloquent communication skills but a great sense of humour.

EYE LINES 

Joy and decision making 

The eye lines are found around your eyes - and can indicate both happiness and stress.

Dadhichi said soft lines that go out from the eye, often called crow's feet, shows a joyful life - but if these lines get too deep, this could be a sign of tension and difficult decision making.

LIFE LINE

Energy, longevity and power

The life lines, commonly known as laugh lines, are creases that appear around the outer corners of your mouth.

'These lines can tell you about your health, life force and longevity,' Dadhichi said.

He explained 'long and evenly formed' life lines will bring you longevity and luck.

'When long, and reaching to your chin, your life lines indicate good luck. If your life lines are long and curve upwards, you will lead and active life in your old age' he said.
