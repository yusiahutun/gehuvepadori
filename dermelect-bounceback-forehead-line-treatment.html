Whether you view wrinkles as a concern or not, one fact remains: They can be difficult to reverse. This especially rings true for particularly stubborn areas, such as the forehead, where 11 lines (the lines between your brows), expression lines, crows feet, and creases above the brows begin to form early on. While you can certainly opt for in-office procedures to minimize their appearance, applying a forehead wrinkle treatment regularly offers its own set of benefits.

This is where Dermelect's newest addition, the Bounceback Forehead Line Treatment, can be of use. The targeted treatment was formulated specifically for sensitive, mature skin, and features top-notch anti-wrinkle ingredients that tackle concerns on the upper region of the face. The gel-like cream instantly provides a blurring, soft-focus effect upon contact with the skin, and quickly gets to work on erasing existing concerns while preventing others from forming.

Shop now: $78; dermelect.com

According to board-certified dermatologist, Dr. Michele Green, there are plenty of factors that contribute to forehead wrinkles. "Overexposure to the sun, facial expressions, such as frowning and smiling, gravity, and genetics all play a potential role in speeding the aging process," she shared with InStyle.

But using skincare that contains elasticity-boosting peptides can make all the difference — and that's what you'll find within this targeted treatment. "[Peptides] are proteins that stimulate the growth of new cells and facilitate cell turnover while you sleep," Dr. Green explained. "Peptides also stimulate the production of collagen. Collagen is a protein that is responsible for the skin's firmness and suppleness."

By implementing these collagen-boosting ingredients back into the skin, users should notice a more youthful, smoother looking complexion as an effect. The brand recommends applying a pearl sized amount of the treatment both morning and night to areas of concern with the included zinc applicator.

While the Bounceback Forehead Line Treatment only recently launched at Dermelect, it's already acquired several fans who've witnessed "amazing results" after just a few uses, and seen their fine lines begin to "disappear."
