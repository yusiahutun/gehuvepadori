Frank Agnello is an American reality television personality and author. Moreover, Frank Agnello is popular for being the grandson of the late John Joseph Gotti Jr. His grandfather John is an American former mafia boss who was the head of the Gambino crime family.

Early Life

Frank Gotti Agnello best known as Frank Agnello was born on the 12th of April, 1990, in Long Island, New York, the United States of America. According to his date of birth, he is 33 years old and holds American nationality. He has a birth sign of Aries and believes in Christianity by religion. Agnello also nicknames ‘Gentle Giant.’

Moreover, he is the youngest son of  Carmine Agnello (father) and Victoria Gotti (mother). Professionally, his father is a mobster who worked for the Gambino crime family. His mother is a writer, reality television personality, and actress.
