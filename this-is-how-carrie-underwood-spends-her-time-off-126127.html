At the Grammy’s earlier in February, Carrie Underwood dropped a bombshell announcement: She was taking a break from singing. After a grueling tour schedule and with an almost 2-year-old son, the busy momma certainly deserves some rest. And now the singer’s finally giving us a peek into how she’s spending her free time.
