Robert Kardashian Sr’s heavenly birthday was paid tribute by his daughter, Kim Kardashian, in an emotional social media post, over 11 years after his death on September 30, 2003.

A psychic delivered a message from Robert Kardashian Sr in the comments of Kim Kardashian‘s tribute, hinting at a certain smell he “loved”. It comes after Kim shared how they loved skiing together.

Kim pays tribute to Robert Kardashian Sr

Kim Kardashian paid tribute to her late father, Robert Kardashian Sr, on his heavenly birthday. She expressed how it would have been his 80th birthday today and would have gone skiing to celebrate.

Following a recent ski holiday, she wrote, “I can’t ski without thinking of you. I’ll always remember the trips and memories you created for us and never ever take those moments for granted.”

“God really blessed us with the most amazing thoughtful, patient, funny, charismatic, loving and kind dad. I sooo wish you were here to meet all of our babies and see the life we created,” Kim added to the post.

Psychic gives his ‘pine smell’ take

A psychic who came across Kim’s tribute post has delivered a message in the comments, claiming that Robert Kardashian Sr encouraged him to “tell Kimberly” about a certain smell.

Gilbert Salas, the psychic, said, “Did he love the smell of fresh pine/pine air freshener. I got a huge whiff of that as I read your post. He said tell Kimberly about the pine air freshener. It looks like a little glass jar.”

Fans are “hoping Kim sees the comment” from the psychic, known as Los Angeles Medium on social media. It comes just over a week after her sister’s ex, Scott Disick, had a psychic reading from Chris Riley.

Kardashian sisters reflect on fond memories

Kim’s sisters Khloe and Kourtney Kardashian, who were featured in the skiing photos as young kids, both reflected on their father with fondness under the Instagram post.

Khloe wrote, “I’m so proud of you for your caption!! You’re getting good keeks!! happy birthday to the best daddy ever,” while Kourtney simply put, “Best daddy in the world 🤍🌹.”

Kim says she “knows he’s somehow behind” the life they created after Robert died. Loved ones left words of condolence, such as, “He is with you everyday and he is so proud of you!!!! Love you!”
