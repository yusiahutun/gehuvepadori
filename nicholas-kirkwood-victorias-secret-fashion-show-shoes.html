Victoria's Secret Fashion Show Shoes For Sale

August 01, 2024
blog

Media Platforms Design Team

BAZAAR has followed along on all things Victoria's Secret Fashion Show, from their workout routines, private flight to London, hair and makeup—and even their boyfriends. This is a fetching group, after all. In the interest of telling the whole story, head to toe, we're here to highlight the ladies' gilded footwear, once again designed by Nicholas Kirkwood—which is especially fitting given all of the show's London-based happenings. The elaborate heels and open, gladiator boots donned by the genetically blessed models are set to go on sale on nicholaskirkwood.com and at the designer's London boutique following the show. We can't promise those stems, but heels like these can certainly help.

Kerry Pieri is Harper's Bazaar's Digital Fashion/Features Director. Kerry oversees the fashion vertical for BAZAAR.com, including launching digital covers, styling celebrity features, and managing recurring features including Brand Watch and My Life in Three Looks. In addition, Kerry edits the “best of” each season, from shoes to bags, denim and coats, and is consistently looking for shifts in the fashion realm to lend a voice. Kerry formerly served as the Editorial Director of  StyleCaster.com and as a producer at Full Frontal Fashion. She lives in New York with her husband Steve, and baby girl Lila Sky.
