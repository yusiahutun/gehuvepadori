Kylie Jenner Wore a Red Minidress With Shoulder Pads

May 16, 2024
blog

Red has undoubtedly been the color of the year (thus far). Case in point? Award show carpets have been littered with gowns in the crimson hue (see: Suki Waterhouse, Margot Robbie, and Selena Gomez), and Taylor Swift has been wearing more of the shade than ever before (to be fair, it is her boyfriend Travis Kelce's team color). Now, Kylie Jenner (with a little help from her daughter Stormi Webster) is bringing red to the front row at Fashion Week.

On Monday, the beauty mogul attended the "Les Sculptures" Jacquemus Fashion Show in Saint-Paul-De-Vence, France, looking ready for Valentine's Day in a head-to-toe scarlet ensemble. On top, Jenner wore a cherry-colored minidress with a diagonal cutout along her chest, a high-neck chiffon overlay that wrapped around the tops of her arms, and statement-making shoulder pads. She styled the frock with matching pointed-toe pumps (adorned with a bow embellishment) and a ruby handbag.

In terms of accessories, Jenner opted for large gold stud earrings, a dainty matching bracelet, and a statement ring. Black cat-eye sunnies completed the look, and Jenner's brunette hair was styled in a voluminous blowout with a deep side-part. For her glam, the founder of Kylie Cosmetics glowed with a dewy complexion, bronzed contour, and glossy beige lips.

Jenner was joined by her little Valentine Stormi (sorry, Timothée!), who sat on her lap while wearing a matching red sweater dress and cardigan tied around her shoulders. This isn't Stormi's first fashion show, let alone her first this month. The mother-daughter duo also attended the Valentino Couture show last week in matching black dresses with fuzz trim. Kylie also made appearances (sans Stormi) at Jean Paul Gaultier and the Maison Margiela show, which she attended with her older sister Kim Kardashian and mother/momager Kris Jenner.
