Meet Jason Roy, American Idol Contestant Haven Madison Dad

American Idol Season 21 had an amazing start to it as the first televised contestant was Haven Madison. In the Las Vegas audition (which aired on February 19), Haven wowed all three judges–Katy Perry, Luke Bryan, and Lionel Richie–with her jaw-dropping original “Fifteen.”

Ahead of her audition, Haven revealed that she virtually grew up on a tour bus because her father, Jason Roy, fronted the Christian rock band Building 429.

Jason accompanied his daughter on acoustic guitar during the audition. As Haven completed her performance, the “Teenage Dreams” singer leaped from her seat and said, “You can have it!.” Lionel followed suit and said, “You can have it right now. In fact, let’s go to the studio right now,” praising Haven’s songwriting abilities. “I think when a 16-year-old can really speak the 16-year-old language that all the other 16-year-olds want to hear, it’s really special,” Luke jumped in on the conversation.

With three yeses, Haven moved on to the next round. In the Hollywood Round, Haven was one of the three contestants competing for a fourth Platinum Ticket alongside Elijah McCormick and Matt Wilson. Although Haven did not get one following a vote at the American Music Award, she chose to work on her songwriting in the first part of Hollywood Week.

Clarksville resident sang an original song “Still Need You,” inspired by her brother Avery’s struggle with mental health and depression. For her final solo performance, Haven sang “Bird Set Free” by Sia, which took her to the Top 26 Finalist. For her Top 26 performance, Haven sang “The Middle” by Zedd, Maren Morris & Grey.

One of the main elements in Haven’s success as a singer/writer is her father, Jason Roy, without question.

On September 26, 2022, Jason shared on his Instagram how much he believes in his daughter. He wrote, “I believe in you when you win. I believe in you when you lose. I’ve believed in you since you took your first breath. Since the moment of your first cry… Keep changing the world for the better one person at a time. I’ll be cheering as you go!,” on the occasion of National Daughters Day.

Jason Roy Band

Haven’s father, Jason Roy, fronts the Christian rock band Building 429. To talk about Building 429, the band released its debut album, Space In Between Us, in 2004. They are best known for producing lyrically-driven, anthemic hit songs with a consistent message of hope.

Building 429 received a 2013 Grammy nomination for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album for their album, We Won’t Be Shaken, which debuted at No.1 on Billboard’s Christian Albums chart. Till Date, Building 429 has also received multiple “Song of the Year” Billboard Music Awards (for “We Won’t Be Shaken” and “Where I Belong”), two BMI “Song of the Year” Awards, a “Best New Artist” GMA Dove Award in 2005, and a 2014 “Group of the Year” KLOVE Fan Award nomination.

Some of the popular hits of Building 429 include Where I Belong, Fear No More, We Won’t Be Shaken, Not Finished Yet, Blessing I Can’t See, to name a few.

Is Jason Roy Still Married To Haven Madison’s Mom?

Jason Roy’s wife is Corti Roy and he is married to her to this day. The two exchanged wedding vows on August 4, 2001.

In his blog dating back to August 7, 2013, Jason wrote, “I remember the wedding rehearsal, the way she walked, the way she talked. She wore a purple outfit and seemed to float around the church hall. She had all of her best friends with her and she was a picture of joy. I knew how lucky I was,” reflecting back on their relationship.

Jason and Corti celebrated their 20th wedding anniversary on August 4, 2021. Making an Instagram post, Jason shared that marrying “@itscort was the most perfect decision I could’ve ever made.”
