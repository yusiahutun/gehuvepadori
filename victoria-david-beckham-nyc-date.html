Victoria Beckham Wore a Three-Piece Suit on a Date With David Beckham

October 03, 2024
blog

If ever there was a piece of fashion advice you were going to take from Victoria Beckham, Ms. Posh herself, let it be wearing a three-piece suit for a casual night out. The designer was spotted out in New York City, holding hands with her husband David Beckham, wearing a black suit with sunglasses, and absolutely putting my late pandemic wardrobe to shame.

The pants were extra long, covering her shoes (would not necessarily recommend in N.Y.C. but then again, I am not a former Spice Girl). She wore the vest as a top under a chic tuxedo jacket. For his part, David wore a blue double-breasted blazer over a white t-shirt with gray-ish slacks.

Last week, Victoria did more than just show off her style prowess, she also essentially gave some insight on her personal "don'ts" list — at Justin Bieber's expense. After the singer gifted her a pair of his Drew crocs, she posted them on Instagram with a poll that asked, "Will I be wearing lilac Crocs?!"

When she shared the results (43% of voters said "Yes," while 57% voted "No"), she wrote, "Well that was close! I think I'd rather die but thank you anyway @justinbieber."
