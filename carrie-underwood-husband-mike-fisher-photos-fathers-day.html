See Carrie Underwood's Father's Day Post to Her Dad and Her Husband, Mike Fisher

March 18, 2024
blog

On Sunday, country singer Carrie Underwood took time out of her busy (and we do mean busy) schedule to wish a Happy Father's Day to her dad, Stephen, and her husband, Mike Fisher.

First up, Carrie shared sweet snaps of her and her dad on her wedding day, as well as photos of her dad and mom together. In her second slide, she gave fans a rare look at her husband and sons, Isaiah and Jacob, together in what appear to be never-before-seen photos.

"I am beyond blessed to have two amazing fathers in my life...my own, of course, and the father of my boys. And today I thank my perfect Father in heaven for them both! I love you, Dad and @mfisher1212 ! And #HappyFathersDay to all the dads out there! #Blessed," she captioned the post.

Fans flooded the comments section with a bunch of heart emojis and well wishes for both Stephen and Mike. "Great pictures happy Father's day to your dad and mister Mike Fisher," one wrote. "Happy Father’s Day to both the love of your life," said another.

Mike, who is a former hockey player, and Carrie got engaged in December of 2009. Less than a year later, in July 2010, the two tied the knot at the Ritz-Carlton resort on Lake Oconee, Georgia. The couple welcomed their first son on February 27, 2015, and four years later, their second son was born on January 23, 2019.

Back in May, Mike wished Carrie and his mom a very Happy Mother's Day. "Grateful for these 2 incredible moms! Happy Mother’s Day," he wrote.

So even though the pair is busy, they clearly know how important it is to make time to acknowledge how much they love each other!
