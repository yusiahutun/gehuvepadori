She's a brunette bombshell and the star of the new Charlie's Angels TV remake.

But Minka Kelly has revealed she was once told she wouldn't be famous without going under the knife. 

The 31-year-old actress has told how she was once offered free plastic surgery to help in her quest for success.

Perfect: Minka Kelly looked stunning as she arrived for her appearance on David Letterman's The Late Show last night

The Charlie's Angels star made the revelation last night during an appearance on The Late Show with David Letterman to promote her new show.

Luckily she turned down the offer, which came from a Playboy Playmate.

'I was 19. She was very seductive and said that she wanted to manage me and that I had a lot of potential,' Kelly said.

'She said that she could get me a job at this doctor's office in return for plastic surgery, which I would need to be a Playboy Playmate.'

'All I needed was lipo, fake breasts, a few veneers and all kinds of stuff,' the actress laughed.

'When I told her that I didn't want that, she said I would never be Playmate of the Year and I said, 'I think that's OK. I'm OK with that.''

Minka also made reference to her recent split from New York Yankees star Derek Jeter after three and a half years.

'He is a tremendous guy,' she said of the 37-year-old baseball player.

As she made her way into the Ed Sullivan theatre in New York for her appearance Minka showed off her model physique in a sleek silver Salvatore Ferragamo dress and taupe coloured pumps.
