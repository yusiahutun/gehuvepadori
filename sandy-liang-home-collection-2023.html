Sandy Liang's Home Collection Is Cool-Girl ApprovedShop It Now

January 29, 2024
blog

Sandy Liang’s bow-adorned fashion shows quickly cemented her brand as the ultimate downtown uniform—which today launched its first-ever home collection. Spotted on celebrities like Emily Ratajkowski, the designer’s eponymous label embraces girlhood dressing with rosette-studded tops, ballerina flats, and micro-mini pleated skirts. Its new home items will complete the downtown fashionista’s home, with key items that elevate entryways and coffee tables.

The collection features 12 new items, including new fashion launches. For the home, you can now purchase the Ribbon Runner, a dark green, almost black rug sporting a pink ribbon detail perfectly sized for narrow New York City hallways. Sandy Liang also has an entryway doormat created with coconut fibers, emblazoned with the phrase “I’m going to be my own kind of princess.” Last is a refillable Zippo lighter engraved with the same sentence, sure to quickly become a staple next to fashion coffee-table books everywhere.

Sandy Liang Ribbon Runner

Sandy Liang Ribbon Runner

This new collection also includes loungewear perfect for the stylish homebody. With gingham-patterned underwear, a frilly apron, comfortable pants and tops, and loose housedresses that could double as nighties, this selection of fashion staples upgrades the classic sweatpants-and-tank look.

As with all of Sandy Liang’s launches to date, these items promise to become collectibles, IYKYK must-haves for fashion obsessives. Though the initial launch was quiet, with only a single Instagram post announcing it, we expect these new products to reach sold-out status in no time. You can shop the entire collection right now and turn your home into a runway show.
