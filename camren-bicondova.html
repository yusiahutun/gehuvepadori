Early Life

Camren Bicondova was born as Camren Renee Bicondova on May 22, 1999, in San Diego, California to Joshua Bicondova and Jessi Bicondova. Nationality wise Bicondova is an American and belongs to the white ethnicity.

She started performing since the age of 6 and later learned jazz-funk and hip-hop in Hawaii after her parents shifted there. She became the "Elite Protege” for The PULSE on Tour dance convention at the age of 11.

Career

Bicondova made her acting debut in 2011 with Disney TV series Shake It Up appearing on one episode as Little Highlighter. Following year, she featured in the dance-drama film Battlefield America which helped her to gain public attention.

The same year, her all-girl dance group 8 Flavahz grabbed the runner-up position in the seventh season of  America's Best Dance Crew. In 2014, when she landed the role in Fox's TV series, Gotham to portray the character of young Selina Kyle, she climbed further towards fame.

Her breakthrough performance on series won her the first Saturn Award nomination in the category of Best Performance by a Younger Actor in a Television Series. The series has four seasons altogether till date, and she was part of all the season.

Besides that, she is actively involved in the social cause too like; The USO, North Shore Animal League America, NOH8 Campaign and Global Citizen Festival.
